About This Website

This website was created to support students at the University of Pretoria by simplifying access to important academic, funding, accommodation, and support information. It is especially helpful for first-year students and NSFAS-funded students.

NSFAS & Student Funding (University of Pretoria)

The University of Pretoria offers financial support through bursaries, scholarships, loans, and the National Student Financial Aid Scheme (NSFAS).

  • NSFAS applications are submitted through the national NSFAS system.
  • Qualifying students receive funding for tuition and allowances.
  • Book allowances are paid directly to students via Fundi.
  • Accommodation, meal, and transport allowances must be applied for via the UP Student Portal.
  • Students must meet academic progression requirements to continue receiving funding.

Important: NSFAS follows the N+1 rule, meaning students are funded for the minimum duration of their qualification plus one additional year.

Academics at the University of Pretoria

Each module at the University of Pretoria carries a specific number of credits. Students must pass a minimum number of credits each academic year to progress.

  • Students are expected to attend lectures, tutorials, and practicals.
  • ClickUP is the official learning management system used for course material and announcements.
  • Failure to meet academic requirements may result in academic exclusion or loss of funding.

Academic rules and progression requirements are outlined in the official UP Academic Regulations. Students are encouraged to consult their faculty for specific requirements.

UP Residences & Accommodation

The University of Pretoria provides various accommodation options for students.

  • On-campus university residences
  • UP-accredited off-campus accommodation
  • Private accommodation near UP campuses

Residence applications are processed through the UP Student Portal. Accommodation is subject to availability and students are encouraged to apply early.
